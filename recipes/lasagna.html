Lasagna

Description

This recipe uses simple ingredients you have on hand, and packs massive flavour! This is an easy recipe. It requires just one pan, one bowl and a 9x13 baking dish. It reheats well and can be made ahead of time. You can freeze lasagna either before or after baking.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese (you can replace this with homemade cottage cheese)
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta/Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water, boil lasagna noodles as per the instructions on the packet.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer and bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
